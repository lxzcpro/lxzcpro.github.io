Hi! I am **Xuting Zhang** (Pronounced: *Shyoo-Ting Jahng*), a third-year undergraduate in **Biological Science** at [Westlake University](https://www.westlake.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. [Jianyang Zeng](https://en.westlake.edu.cn/faculty/jianyang-zeng.html). I also recently spent the Fall 2025 semester as an exchange student at [Duke University](https://duke.edu/). My research broadly lies at the intersection of machine learning and the life sciences (**AI4Science**), where I develop computational tools to advance precision medicine.

I am particularly interested in tackling biological complexity across different scales. On the molecular side, I have worked on **Protein Language Models** with Prof. [Fajie Yuan](https://fajieyuan.github.io/) and explored **geometric deep learning** for virtual drug discovery with Prof. [Rohit Singh](https://biostat.duke.edu/profile/rohit-singh) at Duke. My current work focuses on systems biology, where I analyze **single-cell and spatial transcriptomics** to decode cellular heterogeneity and map the complex gene regulatory networks driving disease progression.
